Yun Zhou

I obtained my Ph.D. degree of Computer Science in QMUL in 2015. My advisors were Prof. Norman Fenton, Prof. Martin Neil and Dr. Timothy Hospedales. During the 4 years Ph.D. study, I worked on Bayesian network learning and Transfer learning.  Before that, I received the master's degree in NUDT in P.R. China in 2011. My master degree's advisor was Prof. Weiming Zhang.
